NEWS

PINNED NEWS • Last updated 05/08/2020
About us

The Boston Film Festival is a gathering event that offers free entertainment for everyone. This Watch it outside Boston edition promotes accessibility to outdoor cinema by projecting 8 blockbusters to help the public discover awesome movies. There is something for every taste!

What? Outdoor film festival

Where? Boston Public Gardens

When? From August 5th to 8th, from 6.00 PM to midnight

Price? Free

LATEST NEWS • 08/08/2020
Info COVID-19

With the ongoing rampup of the COVID-19, the city of Boston has taken additional measures. For your safety we have restricted the venue to 1,500 participants. Make sure to reserve your spot and bring your mask. Read more.

NEWS • 05/08/2020
Event launch

Great news! The Boston Film Festival will take at the Boston Public Garden from August 5th to August 8th. The entrance will also be free. Looking forward to seeing you there.
